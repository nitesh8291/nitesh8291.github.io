About Core Pdf

Core Pdf is your all-in-one PDF utility tool designed to make document management easy and efficient on your Android device.

Key Features:

  • Image to PDF: Convert your photos and gallery images into high-quality PDF documents.
  • Smart Scan: Use your camera to scan documents with edge detection and enhancement.
  • PDF to JPG: Extract pages from PDF files and save them as images.
  • Merge PDF: Combine multiple PDF files into a single document.
  • Docx to PDF: Convert Word documents to PDF with layout preservation.
  • Lock PDF: Secure your sensitive documents with password protection.

We are constantly working to improve Core Pdf and add new features. Thank you for choosing our app!
