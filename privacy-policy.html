Privacy Policy

Last updated: March, 2026

Welcome to Core Pdf. Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our application.

1. Data Collection & Privacy

We do NOT collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Our app is designed to work 100% offline for your security.

  • No Account Required: You don't need to sign up or provide an email to use our app.
  • Local Processing: All PDF conversions (Image to PDF, Word to PDF, etc.) happen directly on your device. Your documents are NEVER uploaded to any server.

2. Permissions We Request

To provide core features, we require the following permissions:

  • Camera Access: Required for the "Smart Scan" feature to capture document images.
  • Storage Access: Required to read your existing documents and save the converted PDF/Word files to your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs that collect user behavior data.

4. Data Security

Since all operations are local, your data is as secure as your device itself. We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest security patches.
